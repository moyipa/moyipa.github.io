About

Vent.com is a website where awareness of problems that many teens is displayed. It is a website where people can anonymously talk about their problems so that they can relieve some of their problems. Others can reply to their comments with sympathy, and to make sure that the website remains positive, there is an automatic text checker that ensures that no negative replies will be posted. In addition to the chatting page, the home page displays statistics that show why it is more important now than ever to have a location where others can support others with similar problems with ensurity of being anonymous. Lastly, there is an about page showing the purpose of the website, business plans, and crediting those who gave their time to help create the website.
